Help

Setting Your Office

First you'll need to set your office, this means your shoutouts can be correctly classified.

/myoffice london|capetown

Shouting Out

During the week shoutout to people who helped you out.

/shoutout @person saved me a ton of time with all the things. Thanks!

Made a mistake? Change the course of history with:

/shoutout undo

Voting

At the end of the week take a look at all the shoutouts on this page and vote on 3 favorites. Click the buttons on this page to build the /vote command. It should to look something like this:

/vote #1 #2 #3

Made another mistake? You can undo the latest vote and clear all votes like this:

/vote undo
/vote clear
