Setting Your Office

First you'll need to set your office, this means your cheers can be correctly classified.

/myoffice london

or:

/myoffice capetown

<%= image_tag('/myoffice.gif', class: 'tutorial-image') %>

Cheering

During the week cheer for people who helped you out.

/cheer @person saved me a ton of time with all the things. Thanks!

Made a mistake? Change the course of history with:

/cheer undo

<%= image_tag('/cheer.gif', class: 'tutorial-image') %>

Voting

At the end of the week take a look at all the cheers on this page and vote on 3 favorites. Click the buttons on this page to build the /vote command. It should to look something like this:

/vote <%= (100..200).to_a.shuffle.take(3).join(' #').prepend('#') %>

<%= image_tag('/vote.gif', class: 'tutorial-image') %>

Made another mistake? You can undo the latest vote and clear all votes like this:

/vote undo
/vote clear
